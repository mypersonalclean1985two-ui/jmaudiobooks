Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 24, 2024

JM Audiobooks ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and web service (collectively, the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you create an account or use our Service, we may collect:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and profile picture (if provided)
  • Authentication Data: Login credentials and authentication tokens
  • Profile Data: Bio, preferences, and settings you configure

1.2 Usage Information

We automatically collect information about how you use our Service:

  • Listening History: Books you've listened to, playback progress, and bookmarks
  • Library Data: Books saved to your library and reading lists
  • Statistics: Listening time, completed books, and streak information
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser type, and app version
  • Log Data: IP address, access times, and pages viewed

1.3 Subscription Information

If you subscribe to our premium service:

  • Payment Information: Processed securely through Apple App Store or Google Play Store (we do not store your payment card details)
  • Subscription Status: Current plan, renewal date, and transaction history

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

2.1 Service Provision

  • Create and manage your account
  • Provide access to audiobooks and features
  • Sync your progress across devices
  • Remember your preferences and settings

2.2 Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our Service
  • Develop new features and functionality
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Optimize performance and user experience

2.3 Communication

  • Send you service-related notifications
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Send updates about new features or content (if you opt-in)
  • Notify you about subscription renewals or payment issues

2.4 Legal Compliance

  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Enforce our Terms of Use
  • Protect against fraud and abuse
  • Resolve disputes

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase Cloud Services, which employs industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Encryption at rest
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication

3.2 Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. You may request deletion of your account and data at any time through the app settings.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4.2 Service Providers

We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our Service:

  • Firebase (Google): Cloud hosting, authentication, and database services
  • Apple/Google: Payment processing for in-app purchases
  • Content Providers: LibriVox and Archive.org for audiobook content

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., court orders, subpoenas).

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Access and Update

You can access and update your personal information at any time through the app's profile settings.

5.2 Data Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your account and all associated data. To do so:

  1. Open the app and go to Settings
  2. Scroll to "Danger Zone"
  3. Select "Delete Account Permanently"

Note: This action is irreversible and will permanently delete all your data.

5.3 Marketing Communications

You can opt-out of promotional emails by adjusting your notification settings in the app or clicking the unsubscribe link in any marketing email.

5.4 California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information we collect and the right to request deletion.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

7. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use local storage and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Keep you logged in
  • Analyze usage patterns
  • Improve Service performance

9. Third-Party Links

Our Service may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending you a notification (for significant changes)

Your continued use of the Service after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: support@jmaudiobooks.com
  • In-App: Settings → Contact Support

12. Data Protection Officer

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), you may contact our Data Protection Officer at: privacy@jmaudiobooks.com

By using JM Audiobooks, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
